Maitreyi Pillai

PhD Student in Political Science

I am a PhD student in Political Science at the University of Texas at Dallas, specializing in international relations, and I expect to complete my degree in May 2027. My research focuses at the crux of International Conflict and Human Rights. In my dissertation I aim to examine the relationship between International conflict and Human rights in the lens of the augment in AI.

Before starting my PhD, I interned for the National Maritime Foundation of India, assisting researchers and Indian navy admiral/s in their projects. Prior to this, I used to be a certified French Language Instructor from Université de Mans & Alliance Française de Bombay and worked in the south of France for a year as a language teaching assistant.

I hold a Master’s in Social Data Analytics Research & Political Science. I also hold a Bachelor of Arts in French Literature.
